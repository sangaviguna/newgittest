TOP TOURIST ATTRACTIONS

Murugan Temple

The state has many temples dedicated to Lord Muruga and some of these are in rarefied environs. The journey to the Aruppadai Veedu of Lord Muruga may be arduous but it has its own rewards

More..

Madurai

The Meenakshi Temple, The Meenakshi Sundareswarar temple (twin temples) is one of the biggest temples in India. The original temple built by Kulasekara Pandyan was in ruins

More..

Velankanni

Velankanni is 12 kms from Nagapattinam. Most important and revered Christian Pilgrim Center of the State. It is dedicated to 'Our Lady of Health' popularly known as ‘Sacred Arogya Matha Church’

More..

Rameswaram

Rameswaram is a small Island and Major Pilgrim Centre in the Gulf of Mannar. It is connected to the mainland by Pamban Bridge. On his return from Ceylon after Ravana Yudhdham in Lanka, Sri Rama offered his repentance prayer to Lord Siva
